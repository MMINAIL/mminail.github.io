One of the more intriguing profile logo badges the author has viewed during his travels, the Southcoast Dispensaries of Brookings - Harbor, Oregon USA is located right off the main drag in downtown Brookings, OR.

A super courteous staff and competent management combine for a super Cannabis Culture experience at the Southcoast Dispensaries of Brookings - Harbor, Oregon USA.

Strains touted by the Southcoast dispensary bud-tender include 'Agent Orange' and 'Jager'.

Happy Hour is from 4:20 pm to 6:20 pm PST (GMT -8) with a normal State of Oregon price range of $5 - $25 USD per gram of herbal medicine ( subject to market change and availability ).
