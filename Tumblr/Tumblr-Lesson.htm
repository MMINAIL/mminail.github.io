Preparation

The first step in our tutorial on how to make 'Ganja Butter' is to invest in and develop your choice of initial genetics.

Next, you will need to secure and maintain the following list of ingredients at your Cannabuds Grow Store.

Thirdly, you must then learn how to subsequently extract the 'trichomes' from your captured plant material in order to successfully blend your 'Ganja Butter'.

Ingredient List

  • a. 3qt saucepan
  • b. (1) stick unsalted Challenge butter
  • c. 3.5 gr medicinal 'crushish'
  • d. cheesecloth, two sheets
  • e. set of small to medium glass bowls
  • f. set of rubber gloves
  • g. kitchen timer
  • h. nylon whisk
  • i. large rubber band
  • j. sheet of plastic wrap
  • k. spice grinder- to turn 'buds' into 'crushish'
  • l. ice bath- a large, wide 4 qt. glass bowl filled w ice + cold water

Instructions

Step #1: First, melt your butter in the saucepan on LOW temperature to avoid burning the 'base'.

  • If your 'base' starts to smell like 'popcorn', then you have the temperature of the burner on too high!

Step #2: Next, toss in your medicinal ingredient after the butter has completely melted.

  • Keep the temperature on LOW and stir your now infused 'base' with a nylon whisk every five minutes or so for approx. 1 hour.
  • The trichomes embedded in the 'crushish' will start to release the phyto-cannabinoids over time at a temperature just below combustion.
  • So, continue to keep the temperature of your burner on LOW.

Step #3: Place the double layer of cheesecloth over your large glass bowl.

  • Anchor the cheesecloth to the glass bowl with a large rubber band around the rim of the bowl.
  • Continue to stir your infused 'base' with the nylon whisk.
  • Stop before reaching the end of the second hour.
  • All of the 'THC', 'CBD', and 'Aromatic Terpenes' as well as the other cannabinoids have now been released by the trichomes.

Step #4: It's time to SLOWLY pour the HOT liquid 'Ganja Butter' over the cheesecloth and into a glass bowl that will accommodate the volume of liquid, and also be small enough to fit inside of your 'ice bath'.

  • If the liquid will not all fit into one bowl, then pour the balance of the liquid into another glass bowl.
  • Using your 'rubber gloves', go ahead and squeeze out any excess liquid from the cheesecloth.
  • Leave any suspended plant material stuck in the cheesecloth to be discarded later.

Step #5: Prepare the 'ice bath' and place the finished, large HOT 'Ganja Butter' glass bowl of refined liquid into the 'ice bath', but DO NOT IMMERSE.

Step #6: Now, place the combo 'ice bath' and 'Ganja Butter' glass bowl inside the refrigerator on the lower shelf.

  • For professional food safety preparation standards, it is important to insure that your prepared foods do 'scoot' through the temperate zone as quickly as possible.
  • This will help to keep your 'Ganja Butter' pure.
  • When the 'Ganja Butter' has hardened inside the refrigerator, and is completely cooled down, you may place a sheet of 'plastic wrap' over the 'glass bowl'.

Step #7: After your butter has hardened, you may scoop it into a nice 'butter kettle' for your table and fridge.

  • Finally, you may now spread your 'Ganja Butter' regular way, or in many 'fancy' ways...as long as you use your 'Ganja Butter' as your primary care physician has recommended.
