Executive Summary

Pre-Application Funds For Missouri Dispensary

Target: Pre-Application Fees for a Missouri Integrated Cultivation, Distillation and Dispensary Application.

Amount: The Medical Marijuana Initiative of North America - International Ltd (MMINAIL), an Arizona Benefit Corporation is seeking total funding of $24,300 USD for the 'Campaign to File an Application for a Combined Cultivation, Manufacturing and Dispensary Facility' in southwest Missouri.

Location: Federal Congressional District Seven (7) of the State of Missouri.

What: The Missouri State Medical Marijuana Constitutional Amendment XIV of #2018 previously known as Amendment 2 from the November #2018 mid-term ballot.

When: Date of Mid-term Election; November 6th, #2018

Note. Missouri State statute dictates that Constitutional Amendments shall take effect thirty (30) days after their respective election unless otherwise indicated in the petition.

Main features of MO Amendment 2

MO Amendment 2 permits ...

    1. a State Licensed Physician (SL-PHY) or
    2. a State Licensed Psychiatrist (SL-PSY)

To recommend Medical Marijuana (MedMj) to a Qualified Patient (QP).

Issue: How to Register a Combined Cultivation, Manufacturing and Dispensary Facility in the State of Missouri

Funds: What are the Cash Requirements to apply for a Missouri Commercial Cannabis License (MCCL)

Triad Bank charges a one-time $2,300 due diligence fee to apply for their Bank Services.

The Missouri Dept of Health and Senior Services (DHSS) charges a one-time $6,000 pre-application fee to apply for a #MedMj dispensary store license.

The Missouri Dept of Health and Senior Services (DHSS) charges a one-time $6,000 pre-application fee to apply for a #MedMj distillation and manufacturing facility license.

The Missouri Dept of Health and Senior Services (DHSS) charges a one-time $10,000 pre-application fee to apply for a #MedMj cultivation facility license.

Note A. The pre-application fees are Non-refundable.

Note B. The pre-application fees do NOT confer preferential treatment.

Total: Total Pre-application Fee Requirement = $24,300

Note. Licenses shall be awarded by the Missouri Dept of Health and Senior Services (DHSS) territorially by Missouri Federal Congressional District.
