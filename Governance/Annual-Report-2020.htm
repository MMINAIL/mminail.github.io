MMINAIL General Minutes Dtd 12-31-2020

File #20373157-2020

Time and Place of Annual Meeting

9:00 pm MST; St Louis, Missouri USA

Given: A quorum of an assembly of one is such a number as is competent to transact business.

A quorum measured and accounted of one exists in Tempe, Arizona USA today with primary Director Robert Weber present.

Robert Weber, now as President of the MMINAIL designates Robert Weber (same) Chairman of the Annual Meeting of the Shareholders of the MMINAIL, this 31st day of December, 2020 at 9:00pm MST.

Robert Weber, as President of the MMINAIL also designates Robert Weber (same) as the Secretary of Recording for this meeting.

Robert Weber, as Treasurer of the MMINAIL enters a change as to the current financial snapshot of the MMINAIL on this date, if any.

Registered Logo Badge

The registered logo badge of the MMINAIL as transferred by the Director, Robert Weber is due for re-registration with the USPTO by March 3rd, 2020 between years Five (5) and Six (6) of the initial registration period.

The cost of re-registration is $225 USD and must be completed prior to the end of year Six (6), between year Five (5) and year Six (6) of the initial registration period starting March 3rd, 2015.

Conveyance

Note. In the event of a transfer of the ownership of the USPTO trademark from Robert Weber, Owner to the Benefit corporate entity of which Mr Weber is the primary and only shareholder, then a formal `Conveyance` will be constructed and entered into the Electronic Trademark Assignment System of the USPTO under the tab named `Entity Conversion`.

Changes from last year so noted.

Appointment of Officers and Directors

A vote on the measure to allow Robert Weber to remain as primary Director of the MMINAIL is now closed and passes unanimously.

Robert Weber is now appointed Chairman of the Board of Directors of the MMINAIL, as well as President, Treasurer, and Secretary for the current year of operation.

Setting of Annual Meeting Date

The next annual meeting of the shareholders of the MMINAIL is set to be held in St Louis, Missouri USA at 9:00 pm on December 31st, 2021.

Closing Remarks

Note A. The corporation is required to file with the Arizona Corporation Commission at a minimum an annual report required under A.R.S. § 10-1622 ($45 filing fee).

The Annual Report for fiscal 2021 is due at the Arizona Corporate Commission offices no later than September 30, 2021.

Note B. If a corporation is designated as a Benefit Corporation at start up, then an annual benefit report is required pursuant to A.R.S. §§ 10-2441 and 10-2442 ($10 filing fee).

Robert Weber, Chairman noting no other topics of discussion on the agenda moves for adjournment of the current session directing the Secretary of Recording to type the general minutes of the meeting and to enter a copy of same into the archives of the MMINAIL.

Session so terminated, meeting adjourned, seal affixed.

11:59 pm MST, December 31st, 2020.
