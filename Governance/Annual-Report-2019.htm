Time and Place of Annual Meeting

9:00 pm MST; Tempe, Arizona USA

Given: A quorum of an assembly of one is such a number as is competent to transact business.

A quorum measured and accounted of one exists in Tempe, Arizona USA today with primary Director Robert Weber present.

Robert Weber, now as President of the MMINAIL designates Robert Weber (same) Chairman of the Annual Meeting of the Shareholders of the MMINAIL, this 31st day of December, 2019 at 9:00pm MST.

Trichometry

The Benefit corporation has registered the name of Trichometry Labs as a Trade Name at the Arizona Secretary of State in the past.

In addition, other Social Media accounts developed by the Benefit corporation hold the name of 'Trichometry' within their respective titles, as well.

For example, the Twitter account of @ Robert Hempaz, PhD Trichometry™ has been well established for several years under the handle of 'hempaz' with the literal meaning of "In Peace" in Portugeuese and "Hemp Arizona" in English.

Further, a Facebook page with the name of 'Trichometry' in its title has been published by the Benefit corporation for the entity Trichometry Labs, as well.

Per our Articles of Incorporation, "The future plans of the corporation include a general public benefit for managing and operating a network of production facilities designed to extract limonene and other terpenoids from herbaceous plant materials."

Recently, in May of #2019, Dun and Bradstreet has made our Director aware of another entity in California that has registered as a Corporation under the name of Trichometry, Inc. as of September of #2018.

The date of incorporation of the subject entity in California comes much later than the establishment of our Trichometry Labs entity in Arizona.

No conflicts of interest have arrived between the two entities as of the ongoing date of this Annual Report.

A vote on the measure to allow Robert Weber to remain as primary Director of the MMINAIL is now closed and passes unanimously.

Setting of Annual Meeting Date

The next annual meeting of the shareholders of the MMINAIL is set to be held in Tempe, Arizona USA at 9:00 pm on December 31st, 2019.

Closing Remarks

Note A. The corporation is required to file with the Arizona Corporation Commission at a minimum an annual report required under A.R.S. § 10-1622 ($45 filing fee).

The Annual Report for fiscal 2019 is due at the Arizona Corporate Commission offices no later than September 30, 2019.

Note B. If a corporation is designated as a Benefit Corporation at start up, then an annual benefit report is required pursuant to A.R.S. §§ 10-2441 and 10-2442 ($10 filing fee).
