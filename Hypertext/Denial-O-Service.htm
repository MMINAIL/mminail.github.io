Denial of Service Defined

Simultaneously, a client or a Bot-net of clients using a nefariously written software program ...

Will attempt to open in excess of 100,000 persistent connections to a server at the same time thus denying legitimate traffic the ability to open up a port via a Get or a Post Http request.
