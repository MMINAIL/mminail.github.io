Concept Library

The goal of the Concept Library at the the MMINAIL is to form a Live Repository of code and presentation pages for our development teams to collaborate upon.

The hub of the Concept Library is based on a combination of Html and GFM landing pages.

This "one-to-many" relationship grants the lead Project Manager of the MMINAIL an easy area to view the contributions of the teams, to collect data pertinent to our reporting requirements, and to review the overall progress of our projects.
