Privacy Policy

Welcome! This is the Privacy Policy page of the Carbon Free Footprint project.

Protecting your private information is our priority.

This 'Statement of Privacy' applies to the the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project and governs data collection and usage.

For the purposes of this 'Privacy Policy', unless otherwise noted, all references to the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project include the following website.

'https://mminail.github.io'

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project originally started out as a news and information company, and as such operates the above subject website.

The passing of the AMA of 2010 with help of the MPP by the voters of Arizona in 2010 enabled the Arizona Dept of Health and Human Services to issue patient-user ID "green" cards with the optional 'Authority To Cultivate' stamped on the front of the AzDES issued "green" card.

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project advocates for a return of the patients' right to cultivate using solar energy that was stripped from us at the advent of the opening of expensive medical dispensaries in the State of Arizona away back in the year 2011.

Collection of Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information from our users.

We may gather other personal or non-personal information in the future at which time this 'Privacy Policy' will be updated.

In the interim, information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project.

This information can include the following 'bytes' of information ...

  • your IP address
  • your browser type
  • domain names
  • access times, and
  • referring website addresses

The collected information is then used for the operation of our service, to maintain the quality of our service, and to provide general statistics regarding visitors to the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project website.

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project encourages you to review the privacy statements of other websites that you may choose to 'link to' from our website.

Please understand how other websites collect, use and share your information.

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project is not responsible for the privacy statements of other websites, or for any other content located on websites not operated by the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project.

Security of Personal Information

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project programatically secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.

Children: Not Yet (13) Years of Age

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen (13).

If you are under the age of thirteen (13), the managers of the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project suggest you ask your parent or guardian for permission to use this website.

Please ask your parent or guardian for permission to use this website if you are under the age of thirteen (13).

Opt-Out and Unsubscribe

We respect your privacy and give you an opportunity to 'opt-out' of receiving announcements of certain informational deliveries.

Users may 'opt-out' of receiving any or all communications from the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project by contacting us via email at cannabuds@live.com

Changes to this Statement

This 'Privacy Policy' is effective retroactive to the date of the final counting of the votes during the midterm election of November 2010.

The 'Carbon Free Footprint' project will occassionally update this 'Statement of Policy' to reflect company and customer feedback.

Therefore, the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project encourages you to periodically review this 'Statement of Policy' in order to stay informed of how the 'Carbon Free Footprint' project is protecting your information.
