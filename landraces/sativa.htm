Sativa Article

Welcome! This is the Sativa page of the Carbon Free Footprint project.

Every cell in each Cannabis plant contains 800 million base-pairs (bps) of DNA wrapped within ten (10) separate yet identicle and intertwinable chromosomes.

During the dance of meiosis, new gametes are spawned that share base-pair combinations of the ten (10) parental chromosomes.
