The Aloe plant ( when eaten ) acts as a purgative.

Cleansing Power

In addition to the intestines, the largest leaves of an Aloe plant when added four (4) to a pitcher and diced and sliced and chopped and pureed and mixed with your favorite fruit juice cleanses not only the stomach, but the liver and the lymphatic system, too!

The author suggests frozen mango chunks as a toss-in to the blender, as well.

Warning. This herb has been known to expel pinworms.
