The Annual Report

Each September, at the end of the month the Annual Report of the Benefit Corp comes due.

The cost to file the annual report with the Secretary of State is $45 USD.

Why not pay that expense forward for the corporation?

Your donation of $450 will ensure the annual report is submitted in a timely fashion without the added surprise of monthly late penalties of $9 per month ( in Arizona ).
