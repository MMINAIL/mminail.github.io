MDX Medcoin Ticker

The "ticker" symbol of MDX was once used to denote a McDonnell Douglas DC-10 Jet Passenger Plane.

Adoption

The Board of Director of the MMINAIL adopt the ticker symbol MDX for the crypto currency commonly known as Medcoin™ in the Great State of Arizona, pending additional search for same amongst the currently prevailing set of tradeable crypto currencies.
